## Key Takeaways

### ‚úÖ What Works

1. **Hybrid model** aligns with crypto culture ("hold to access")
2. **Transaction tax** provides recurring revenue without subscriptions
3. **Premium tier** captures high-value users (10% adoption = 40% of profit)
4. **Revenue sharing** incentivizes Top 100 holders to accumulate and hold
5. **Optimized pricing** ($199 vs $150) improves margins by 32%

### ‚ö†Ô∏è What's Risky

1. **Continuous growth required**: Need 120+ new users/month minimum
2. **High churn** (20%): Must improve retention or growth offsets losses
3. **Token volatility**: 40% price swings affect user access (threshold management)
4. **Competitive pressure**: Other AI utility tokens launching

### ‚ùå What Doesn't Work

1. **One-time purchase model**: Unsustainable at scale (revenue collapses)
2. **Monthly subscriptions**: Misaligned with token holder expectations
3. **Relying on tax only**: Insufficient revenue without new user growth
